FAQ

Common  questions

What age groups do you accept?

We welcome children from 3 months to 6 years old, with age-appropriate programs tailored to their developmental needs.

What are your school hours?

Our nursery operates from 8am to 5pm, Monday to Friday. We also offer early drop-off and late pick-up options for added flexibility.

How do you ensure the safety of the children?

Safety is our top priority. We have secure entry systems, CCTV monitoring, trained staff in first aid, and a low student-teacher ratio to ensure close supervision at all times.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we offer healthy, balanced meals and snacks throughout the day. We also accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies to ensure every child’s needs are met.
